1 # avrSerial
2 
3 This is a serial library for many different Atmel AVR MCUs. It is using two FIFO Buffers per USART module for interrupt driven UART communication. XON/XOFF Flow control for incoming data can be enabled for all UART modules, it will operate independently for each.
4 
5 Device-specific configuration is in serial_device.h. You should be able to easily add new AVR MCUs. Just get the relevant register and bit names from the data-sheet.
6 
7 A small test application is included. It will be built when calling either of these commands
8 
9  make all
10  make test.hex
11 
12 You can also flash it with
13 
14  make program
15 
16 just adjust your programmer type and port in the makefile.
17 
18 For normal use, either include serial.c, serial.h and serial_device.h in your project, or build a statically-linked library by calling
19 
20  make lib
21 
22 and linking this to your project, as well as including serial.h.
23 
24 The current Doxygen Documentation can be found in doc/ and [on the web](http://www.xythobuz.org/avrserial/) or as [PDF](http://www.xythobuz.org/avrserial.pdf)...
